## What is ITROPA?

ITROPA helps you discover innovative opportunities by exploring the intersection of human needs and existing solutions. Instead of inventing from scratch, ITROPA shows you how to recombine proven mechanisms from different industries to create breakthrough innovations.

### Why Use ITROPA?

**Avoid reinventing the wheel.** Most innovations are recombinations of existing ideas applied to new contexts. ITROPA helps you:

- **Identify fundamental human needs** that drive markets and behaviors
- **Discover proven solutions** from different industries and time periods
- **Generate future predictions** by combining mechanisms in novel ways
- **Visualize innovation pathways** as branching trees of possibilities
- **Cross-pollinate ideas** from unrelated domains
- **Build a personal library** of mechanisms and patterns

## Exploring Prior Art

Prior Art shows you how others have addressed this need before. This is your innovation goldmine.

### Four Categories of Prior Art

**1. Current Leaders**
Modern solutions dominating the market today.
- **Example:** For "Belonging" ‚Üí Social media platforms, online communities
- **Why it matters:** See what's working now and why
- **What to extract:** Successful mechanisms you can adapt

**2. Historical Precedents**
Solutions from the past that addressed the same need.
- **Example:** For "Belonging" ‚Üí Town squares, coffee houses, guilds
- **Why it matters:** Time-tested approaches that still resonate
- **What to extract:** Enduring patterns that transcend technology

**3. Adjacent Domains**
Solutions from different industries addressing similar challenges.
- **Example:** For "Belonging" ‚Üí Team sports, religious communities, coworking spaces
- **Why it matters:** See transferable mechanisms from unexpected places
- **What to extract:** Cross-industry patterns ready for new contexts

**4. Nature Solutions**
Biological systems that solve analogous problems.
- **Example:** For "Belonging" ‚Üí Bee colonies, wolf packs, mycorrhizal networks
- **Why it matters:** Nature has optimized solutions over millions of years
- **What to extract:** Biomimicry opportunities

### Using Prior Art Effectively

**Toggle the Prior Art Panel:**
- Click "View Prior Art" to expand the panel
- Read through all four categories
- Look for surprising connections
- Identify transferable mechanisms

**Key Questions to Ask:**
- What core mechanism makes this solution work?
- Why did it work in its original context?
- What constraints or limitations did it have?
- How might this transfer to a new domain?

## Advanced Features

### 1. Extract Mechanisms

**What it does:** Identifies the core pattern that makes a solution work, abstracting it from its specific implementation.

**When to use it:**
- You want to understand WHY something works
- You're looking for transferable patterns
- You need ideas for cross-pollination

**How to use it:**
1. Right-click any industry/prediction
2. Select "Extract Mechanism"
3. Wait for AI analysis (15-30 seconds)
4. Review the mechanism breakdown

**What you'll see:**
- **Core Mechanism** - The fundamental pattern
- **Abstract Pattern** - Universal principles
- **Historical Applications** - Where it's been used before
- **Untapped Domains** - New opportunities
- **Combination Potential** - How to mix with other mechanisms

**AI Assistant Integration:**
The mechanism analysis includes intelligent suggestions:
- Find similar mechanisms in your library
- Apply the pattern to new domains
- Compare with related mechanisms
- Identify combination opportunities

### 2. Deep Dive Analysis

**What it does:** Performs market analysis on a predicted industry to evaluate its viability and opportunity.

**When to use it:**
- You're evaluating business opportunities
- You need market sizing information
- You want to understand barriers to entry
- You're preparing for strategic planning

**How to use it:**
1. Right-click any future prediction
2. Select "Deep Dive"
3. Wait for comprehensive analysis
4. Review market insights

**What you'll see:**
- **Market Opportunity** - Size and potential
- **Key Enablers** - What makes this possible now
- **Challenges & Solutions** - Barriers and how to overcome them
- **Timeline** - When this will likely emerge
- **First Mover Advantage** - Benefits of early entry
- **Prior Art Leverage** - Existing solutions to build upon
- **Key Players** - Who's positioned to win
- **Risks** - What could go wrong

**AI Assistant Integration:**
Deep dive analysis includes actionable suggestions:
- Compare with adjacent markets
- Find related opportunities
- Identify strategic partners
- Extract competitive advantages

### 3. Branching (Creating Sub-Industries)

**What it does:** Creates specialized variations or niche markets within a broader industry.

**When to use it:**
- Exploring specific market segments
- Drilling down into niches
- Creating specializations
- Mapping industry evolution

**How to use it:**
1. Right-click a prediction (can't branch past predictions)
2. Select "Branch"
3. Enter details for your sub-industry:
   - **Name** - What the niche is called
   - **Mutation** - How it differs from parent
   - **Insight** - Why this matters
4. Click "Add Prediction"

### 4. Cross-Pollination

**What it does:** Combines mechanisms from two different industries to generate novel hybrid innovations.

**When to use it:**
- Looking for breakthrough innovations
- Combining complementary solutions
- Creating unique value propositions
- Exploring convergence opportunities

**How to use it:**
1. Select multiple industries using checkboxes
2. Click "Cross-Pollinate" button
3. Wait for AI to generate hybrid concepts
4. Review the generated combinations
5. Click "Add to Need" to save promising hybrids

**What makes good cross-pollination:**
- Industries from different needs
- Complementary mechanisms
- Non-obvious combinations
- Solving different aspects of a problem

**Example Cross-Pollination:**
```
Gamification (from Mastery)
  + Social Validation (from Status)
  = Social Learning Platforms with Achievement Systems
```

**Drag & Drop Cross-Pollination:**
You can also drag one industry onto another to quick-add both to cross-pollination queue.

### 5. AI-Powered Suggestions

**What it does:** Provides intelligent, context-aware recommendations based on your current analysis.

**When you'll see it:**
- After extracting a mechanism
- After running a deep dive
- During cross-pollination
- When viewing patterns

**Understanding AI Suggestions:**

**Each suggestion includes:**
- **Action** - What the AI recommends
- **Reasoning** - Why this is suggested
- **One-Click Execution** - Perform the action immediately

**Types of Suggestions:**
- Create new need exploration
- Compare related mechanisms
- Branch into suggested niches
- Find similar patterns
- Extract transferable mechanisms
- Apply patterns to new domains

**Using Suggestions:**
- **Execute Individual** - Click the play button on any suggestion
- **Execute All** - Click "Run All Actions" to batch execute
- **Review First** - Read the reasoning before executing

**Suggestions are contextual:**
- Mechanism analysis ‚Üí Pattern matching, domain transfer
- Deep dive ‚Üí Market comparison, opportunity analysis
- Cross-pollination ‚Üí Hybrid variations, combination ideas

### Troubleshooting

**Search returns no results:**
- Try simpler, more fundamental needs
- Use single words instead of phrases
- Try synonyms (e.g., "community" instead of "belonging")
- Ensure you have an internet connection

**AI is taking a long time:**
- Complex analyses can take 15-60 seconds
- Don't refresh - wait for completion
- Multiple requests queue automatically
- Check browser console for errors

**Can't find my saved work:**
- Check the Library view
- Use search to find specific items
- Data is stored locally in your browser
- Clearing browser data will delete library

**Cross-pollination not working:**
- Ensure you've selected 2+ items
- Both items must have checkboxes selected
- Wait for previous operations to complete
- Try simpler source industries

**Deleted something by accident:**
- Use Ctrl+Z immediately to undo
- Check undo history for recent actions
- Library deletions cannot be undone
- Export important work regularly

## Understanding Your Data

### Where Your Data Lives

**Local Storage:**
All your research is saved locally in your browser using IndexedDB:
- Explored needs and their timelines
- Extracted mechanisms
- Deep dive analyses
- Cross-pollination results
- Search cache for faster loading

**Privacy:**
- No data is sent to external servers (except AI API calls)
- Your research remains private
- Clear browser data to remove everything
- Export to keep backups

### Exporting Your Work

**From the Home Screen:**
1. Select a need tab you want to export
2. Click the Export dropdown
3. Choose your format:

**JSON** - Complete data structure
- All predictions with full details
- Mechanisms and deep dives
- Relationships and inspirations
- Best for backup or data processing

**Markdown** - Human-readable documentation
- Formatted for easy reading
- Includes all insights
- Ready for note-taking apps
- Best for sharing and presentations

**CSV** - Spreadsheet format
- Flat table of all predictions
- Era, name, mutation, insight columns
- Import into Excel or Google Sheets
- Best for analysis and filtering

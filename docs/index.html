What is ITROPA?

ITROPA helps you discover innovative opportunities by showing you how existing solutions from different industries can be recombined to address human needs in new ways.

Core Philosophy Innovation = Prior Art + New Context. Most innovations aren't created from scratch—they're recombinations of existing solutions applied in new ways.

How It Works

  1. Explore Human Needs

    Start with fundamental needs like Belonging, Status, or Mastery

  2. Research Prior Art

    See how needs have been met historically and across domains

  3. Generate Predictions

    AI predicts future industries addressing these needs

  4. Extract Mechanisms

    Understand WHY solutions work at an abstract level

  5. Cross-Pollinate

    Combine mechanisms from different domains for breakthroughs

Getting Started

Get up and running with ITROPA in just 5 minutes. Follow these simple steps to start discovering innovative opportunities.

Step 1: Choose a Human Need

Click one of the suggested needs on the home screen or search for your own. Popular needs include:

  • Belonging - Connection with others
  • Status - Recognition and achievement
  • Mastery - Skill development
  • Security - Safety and stability
  • Love - Deep connection
  • Sustainability - Long-term wellbeing

Step 2: Explore Prior Art

Click "View Prior Art" to see how this need has been addressed before:

Current Leaders

Solutions dominating the market today

Historical

Time-tested solutions from the past

Adjacent Domains

Solutions from other industries

Nature

Biomimicry opportunities

Step 3: Review Future Predictions

Scroll through the timeline to see AI-generated predictions for the Post-AI Era (2025+). The timeline displays how industries may evolve as technologies and social patterns shift, showing predictions that span from near-term opportunities to long-term transformations.

Step 4: Try an Action

Right-click any prediction and choose an action to deepen your understanding.

Pro Tip Start with "Extract Mechanism" to understand the core patterns that make solutions work. This forms the foundation for cross-pollination.

Advanced Features

Identifies the core pattern that makes a solution work, abstracting it from its specific implementation.

When to use: Understanding WHY something works, looking for transferable patterns, or need ideas for cross-pollination.

Deep Dive Analysis

Performs market analysis on a predicted industry to evaluate its viability and opportunity.

When to use: Evaluating business opportunities, need market sizing information, understanding barriers to entry.

Branching

Creates specialized variations or niche markets within a broader industry.

When to use: Exploring market segments, drilling down into niches, creating specializations.

Cross-Pollination

Complete User Guide

Working with Human Needs

Searching for Needs

  1. Click the search bar at the top
  2. Type a human need (e.g., "belonging", "creativity", "healthcare")
  3. Press Enter or click the search icon
  4. Wait while AI researches the need (10-30 seconds)

Managing Multiple Needs

  • Search for different needs to open them as tabs
  • Click tab headers to switch between needs
  • Use arrow keys (← →) to navigate tabs
  • Click × button to close tabs

Understanding Prior Art

Prior Art shows you how others have addressed this need before. This is your innovation goldmine.

Key Questions to Ask
  • What core mechanism makes this solution work?
  • Why did it work in its original context?
  • What constraints or limitations did it have?
  • How might this transfer to a new domain?

Using the Library

Your personal knowledge repository of everything you've explored.

  • Statistics Dashboard: Track your research progress
  • Recent Activity: See latest explorations chronologically
  • Search: Find specific items in your research history
  • Filter by Type: Focus on needs, mechanisms, deep dives, or cross-pollinations

Pattern Library

Discover recurring patterns across all your explorations.

  • Abstract Patterns: Universal principles that appear in multiple industries
  • Pattern Instances: Each place this pattern appears with specific implementations
  • Cross-Domain Insights: See which mechanisms work reliably across different contexts

Tips & Best Practices

Start Broad, Then Specialize

Begin with fundamental human needs, review all prior art categories thoroughly, then use branching to drill into specific niches.

Extract Mechanisms Liberally

Extract mechanisms from surprising sources. Look for patterns across different needs. Build your pattern library for deeper insights.

Cross-Pollinate Strategically

Combine industries from different needs. Look for complementary mechanisms. Try non-obvious combinations for breakthroughs.

Use AI Suggestions

Read the reasoning before executing. Batch execute related suggestions. Follow up on recommended explorations.

Organize Your Library

Regularly review your saved needs. Delete outdated explorations. Search your library before creating duplicates.

Think in Mechanisms

Focus on WHY something works. Abstract beyond specific implementations. Look for transferable principles.

Troubleshooting

Search returns no results

  • Try simpler, more fundamental needs
  • Use single words instead of phrases
  • Try synonyms (e.g., "community" instead of "belonging")
  • Ensure you have an internet connection

AI is taking a long time

  • Complex analyses can take 15-60 seconds
  • Don't refresh - wait for completion
  • Multiple requests queue automatically

Can't find my saved work

  • Check the Library view
  • Use search to find specific items
  • Data is stored locally in your browser
  • Clearing browser data will delete library
Data Storage Warning Your data is stored locally in your browser. Export important work regularly and avoid clearing browser storage if you want to preserve your research.

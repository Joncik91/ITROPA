What is ITROPA?

ITROPA helps you discover innovative opportunities by showing you how existing solutions from different industries can be recombined to address human needs in new ways.

Core Philosophy: Innovation = Prior Art + New Context

Most innovations aren't created from scratch‚Äîthey're recombinations of existing solutions applied in new ways. ITROPA makes this process systematic and AI-powered.

How It Works

  1. Explore Human Needs - Start with fundamental needs like Belonging, Status, or Mastery
  2. Research Prior Art - See how needs have been met historically and across domains
  3. Generate Predictions - AI predicts future industries addressing these needs
  4. Extract Mechanisms - Understand WHY solutions work at an abstract level
  5. Cross-Pollinate - Combine mechanisms from different domains for breakthroughs

üöÄ Getting Started (5 Minutes)

Step 1: Choose a Human Need (30 seconds)

Click one of the suggested needs on the home screen or search for your own:

  • Belonging - Connection with others
  • Status - Recognition and achievement
  • Mastery - Skill development
  • Security - Safety and stability
  • Love - Deep connection

Click "View Prior Art" to see how this need has been addressed before:

  • Historical Precedents - Time-tested solutions
  • Adjacent Domains - Solutions from other industries
  • Nature Solutions - Biomimicry opportunities

Step 3: Review Future Predictions (1 minute)

Scroll through the timeline to see AI-generated predictions:

  • Near Future - 5-10 years out
  • Far Future - 10-25 years
  • Distant Future - 25+ years

Step 4: Try an Action (2 minutes)

Right-click any prediction and choose:

  • Extract Mechanism - Understand WHY it works
  • Deep Dive - Analyze the market opportunity
  • Branch - Create specialized sub-categories

Step 5: Cross-Pollinate (30 seconds)

  1. Check boxes next to 2+ different industries
  2. Click "Cross-Pollinate"
  3. Review AI-generated hybrid innovations
  4. Add promising ones to your need

  • Click tab headers to switch between needs
  • Use arrow keys (‚Üê ‚Üí) to navigate tabs

Understanding Prior Art

Prior Art shows you how others have addressed this need before. This is your innovation goldmine.

Four Categories

  • Current Leaders: Modern solutions dominating the market today
  • Historical Precedents: Solutions from the past that addressed the same need
  • Adjacent Domains: Solutions from different industries addressing similar challenges
  • Nature Solutions: Biological systems that solve analogous problems
Key Questions to Ask:
  • What core mechanism makes this solution work?
  • Why did it work in its original context?
  • What constraints or limitations did it have?
  • How might this transfer to a new domain?

Advanced Features

Extract Mechanisms

Identifies the core pattern that makes a solution work, abstracting it from its specific implementation.

When to use: You want to understand WHY something works, looking for transferable patterns, or need ideas for cross-pollination.

Deep Dive Analysis

Performs market analysis on a predicted industry to evaluate its viability and opportunity.

When to use: Evaluating business opportunities, need market sizing information, understanding barriers to entry, or preparing for strategic planning.

Branching

Creates specialized variations or niche markets within a broader industry.

When to use: Exploring market segments, drilling down into niches, creating specializations, or mapping industry evolution.

Cross-Pollination

Combines mechanisms from different industries to generate novel hybrid innovations.

When to use: Looking for breakthrough innovations, combining complementary solutions, creating unique value propositions, or exploring convergence opportunities.

üí° Tips & Best Practices

Start Broad, Then Specialize

Begin with fundamental human needs, review all prior art categories thoroughly, then use branching to drill into specific niches.

Extract Mechanisms Liberally

Extract mechanisms from surprising sources. Look for patterns across different needs. Build your pattern library for deeper insights.

Cross-Pollinate Strategically

Combine industries from different needs. Look for complementary mechanisms. Try non-obvious combinations for breakthroughs.

Use AI Suggestions

Read the reasoning before executing. Batch execute related suggestions. Follow up on recommended explorations.

Organize Your Library

Regularly review your saved needs. Delete outdated explorations. Search your library before creating duplicates.

Think in Mechanisms, Not Features

Focus on WHY something works. Abstract beyond specific implementations. Look for transferable principles.

Leverage Historical Prior Art

Don't ignore old solutions. Time-tested mechanisms often resurface. Combine old patterns with new technology.

Document Your Insights

Use the export feature for important discoveries. Create branches for detailed explorations. Build your personal innovation framework.

‚ùì Troubleshooting

Search returns no results

  • Try simpler, more fundamental needs
  • Use single words instead of phrases
  • Try synonyms (e.g., "community" instead of "belonging")
  • Ensure you have an internet connection

AI is taking a long time

  • Complex analyses can take 15-60 seconds
  • Don't refresh - wait for completion
  • Multiple requests queue automatically

Can't find my saved work

  • Check the Library view
  • Use search to find specific items
  • Data is stored locally in your browser
  • Clearing browser data will delete library

Data not saving

  • Check browser storage permissions
  • Storage quota may be full
  • Try different browser
  • Export important work regularly
